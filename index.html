Team Mission & Vision Statement

Our mission is to inspire and engage our community, especially the FIRST community, in fostering innovation and a STEM-driven culture. We prioritize gender equality in STEM, guided by FIRST Core Values, and welcome students to participate in our projects spanning STEM and social causes .)

Past Awards & Recognitions of #6431 in FIRST .)

In the 2023 season, we were honored to receive the FIRST Impact Award in the Istanbul Regional, the Judges Award and the Woodie Flowers Finalist Award in the Bosphorus regional with our beloved mentor Sedat Yalcin.

In the 2025 season, we won the FIRST Impact Award at the Haliç Regional and the Engineering Inspiration Award at the Bosphorus Regional.

This season, we participated in the Bist Başakşehir Off-season. We won the Quality Award, Dean's List Award, and Finalist Award. We hope for a beautiful season to follow these successes.)
